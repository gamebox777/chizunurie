## 2. Information we collect

1. **Account information**: If you sign in with Google, your email address,
   display name, and profile image. If you play as a guest (anonymous) without
   registering, only an anonymous identifier tied to the device is issued.
2. **Location (GPS)**: Only when you use the "Visit paint" feature, your device
   location is read to determine which ~1km grid tile to paint. The location of
   painted tiles is stored as gameplay progress. Location use is optional; you
   can still play by painting the map manually without granting permission.
3. **Gameplay data**: Painted tiles, painted municipalities/regions, points,
   levels, experience, play time, and similar.
4. **Logs and access data**: Operation logs (sign-in, GPS use, search), access
   timestamps, IP address, device/browser information, and a hash derived from
   the IP (used for access counting).
5. **Cookies and similar technologies**: Used to keep you signed in and for
   analytics (advertising applies to the browser version only; **the app shows
   no ads**).

## 3. Purposes

- Providing and operating the Service (painting, saving progress, rankings)
- Saving gameplay progress and carrying it over to other devices
- Detecting and addressing abuse and bugs, and improving the Service
- Understanding access (analytics)
- Delivering and displaying ads

## 4. Third-party services

The Service uses the following Google services, which receive information within
their respective purposes. Each provider handles data per its own policy.

- **Google Sign-In (OAuth)**: for authentication.
- **Google Analytics**: for usage analytics (usage, device info, identifiers).
- **Google AdSense / Google Ads (including rewarded ads)**: for advertising.
  **Used on the browser version only, not in the app.**
  - How Google uses information for ads: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  - Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 5. Advertising

**The app shows no ads.** The browser (web) version contains ads (Google AdSense and
rewarded video ads); third parties (such as Google) may use cookies and identifiers
to deliver interest-based ads. You can turn off ad personalization at
[Google Ads settings](https://myadcenter.google.com/).

## 6. Disclosure to third parties

Except as required by law, we do not provide collected information to third
parties without consent. Transmission to the third-party services above occurs
within the scope of your agreement to this policy.

## 7. Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes above, or
until you request deletion.

## 8. Data deletion and your rights

## 9. Children's privacy
